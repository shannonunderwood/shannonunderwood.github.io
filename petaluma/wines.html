OUR WINES


AT PETALUMA, OUR MISSION IS TO HANDCRAFT SMALL BATCHES OF EXQUISITE WINES.

To us that means our wines are focused on elegance and finesse, the benchmarks of what we consider to be the very heart and soul of Petaluma. Our fruit is sourced from our backyard, one of the finest vineyards in Sonoma Valley.

Our History


Our founders love of Californian wine runs deep. Sonoma is one of the most sought after wine regions in the US and we are dedicated to bring a unique experience to our guests. Petaluma boutique wines started back in 2005 and gets it’s name from our lovely town. We take pride in our wine and we handle it with extreme care. Lot’s of love and hard work goes into our small-batch boutique wines, making them uniquely special and one of a kind. We provide a modern and stylish tasting room, with rotating food trucks, approved to pair well with our wines. Live local music and other exciting events take place weekly. Placed in the rolling hills of Sonoma County, we have become a destination you won’t want to miss. We hope you enjoy our wines and location as much as we do.
