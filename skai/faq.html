FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS



Q: HOW SAFE ARE THESE DRONES?

Answer

Each drone is flown by a drone certified pilot who must complete at least 2 years of drone training. In case of an emergency, the drones are equipped with an advanced backup automated piloting system to guarantee your safety. Parachutes are also within easy reach of every seat.

Q: HOW FAR CAN THESE THINGS GO?

Answer

Skai drones are designed to carry you safely and quickly to your destination. They are not designed to carry you over extreme distances. Most drones cover a max of a 50 mile radius.

Q: HOW FAST DO THEY FLY?

Answer

Skai drones can reach speeds of up to 200 miles per hour.

Q: WHAT ARE MY PAYMENT OPTIONS?

Answer

Any electronic method of payment will work. Log in to your Skai account and select billing. There you can link your bank account, credit card, venmo, or paypal information.
